    YinJAR

    Not much here, it's more a "tech demo at the moment." But, you can run around, pickup items and kill monsters.

    System Requirements:
    • Python 2.7 (not 3.x) from http://python.org
    • PyYaml from http://pyyaml.org

    Key Reference (no help screen at the moment):

    • Arrow Keys for movement / selecting
    • Mouse hover to see the item at the location
    • 'i' to see inventory
    • 'd' to drop an item
    • 'g' to pick up an item
    • '>' to go down a level
    • '<' to go up a level

    Monster Reference:

    • red 'i': Imp, annoying but quite harmless
    • solid blue smiley-face: Orc, Just a bit more dangerous
    • orange 'h': Human warrior, dangerous in groups
    • orange smiley-face: Troll
    • amber smiley-face: Olog-Hai (from Tolkien), quite dangerous
    • amber '%': Troll Warrior, extremely dangerous

    Item Reference:

    • purple heart: health potion, restores 10 HP
    • yellow heart: super health potion, gives you a chance to restore 10 HP and increase max HP by 10
    • dark red 'c': confuse, make nearest monster attack its friends for a time
    • green 's': strengthen, give 3 damage boost to player for a while
    • green 'p': protect, give 6 defense boost for a while
    • dark red 'z': lightning bolt, inflict 13 damage on nearest monster
    • dark green 'j': jump, move up to 3 spaces effortlessly, or try to move further with possible health penalty
    • dark green 'a': acquire, select and pick up an item within 5 spaces of you
    • red 'starburst': smite, select and inflict 10 damage upon target
    • dark red '*': fireball, select and blast a target and all enemies within 5 spaces of him
